1. Get the ClickHouse Admin Password:
   echo "Admin Password: $(kubectl get secret {{ include "clickhouse.fullname" . }}-admin -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -o jsonpath='{.data.admin}' | base64 --decode)"

2. Check the status of your ClickHouse Installation:
   kubectl get chi {{ include "clickhouse.fullname" . }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

3. Monitor the Pods:
   kubectl get pods -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l clickhouse.altinity.com/chi={{ include "clickhouse.fullname" . }}

4. View ClickHouse Logs:
   kubectl logs -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} chi-{{ include "clickhouse.fullname" . }}-main-0-0-0 -c clickhouse

5. Secret Name for Reference:
   {{ include "clickhouse.fullname" . }}-admin
